General Guidelines

  • Each participant should carry identity proof (i.e., College ID Card or the authorization letter).
  • All participants must carry their respective college ID cards within the college premises.
  • All team members should be from the same college/institute.
  • Participants can register online before the event date.
  • A participant can participate in only one event at a time.
  • Modifications to teams are not permitted after registration.
  • All events will commence at the scheduled time. Punctuality will be appreciated.
  • If the timing changes, it will be informed via WhatsApp or mail.
  • Participants must report 30 minutes before the commencement of their events.
  • Rules may change at the discretion of the organizers.
  • Each event will have specific rules and regulations. Participants must adhere to them.
  • Plagiarism is strictly prohibited and leads to disqualification if found guilty.
  • Participants should carry pen drives and laptops for their personal use.
  • File submissions (pictures, videos, audio) should be in the prescribed format.
  • Delayed submissions will not be entertained.
  • The registration fee is Rs.500, inclusive of food, refreshments, and certificates.
  • Accommodation will be provided for outstation participants. Late bookings will be arranged in nearby hotels.
  • Participants must take care of their belongings. Organizers are not responsible for any loss.
  • Outside food is not allowed within the premises.
  • Smoking, drinking, and using objectionable substances are strictly prohibited and will lead to disqualification.
  • Vulgar language or offensive behavior will result in immediate disqualification.
  • The judge’s decision will be final and binding in all circumstances.
  • Participants should arrange travel back home at the end of the event.
  • The committee reserves the right to modify the schedule or platform if necessary.
  • Certificates of Participation will be awarded to all participants.
  • The best possible support will be provided during technical glitches, but participants assume their own risk.
